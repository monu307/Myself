Bright Future Teaching Assistant (TA) Program

We are building Bright Future with a core mission of helping people unlock their true potential so they could land the better tech jobs they actually deserve. We cannot do it just by ourselves - we are looking for the like-minded people who believe in the same mission.

Teaching Assistants are a crucial part of the Bright Future community - they are helping students to achieve their goals by coaching them on the program material and making sure that the students are on the correct learning path.
